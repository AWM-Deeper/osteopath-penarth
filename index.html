About Your Osteopath

David Langdon

BSc (Hons) Osteopathy — GOsC Registered

David moved back to Wales in 2019 after living in London for 30 years, and now practises in Penarth from Old Penarthians RFC and at The Vale Foot Clinic on High Street, Barry.

After spending 20 years as a Medical Photographer working in hospitals around the UK — including 10 years at King's College Hospital — he retrained as an Osteopath at the London School of Osteopathy, graduating in 2008 with a BSc (Hons) in Osteopathy. He then opened his own clinic and gym, Langdon Osteopathy, near London's Canary Wharf, which he ran until moving to Penarth.

David is also qualified as an Active Release Technique (Spine) therapist, a western acupuncture practitioner, qualified in Kinesio Taping, food intolerance testing, and is a Poliquin Certified Personal Trainer and Kinetic Link Training (KLT) instructor.

David's main interest outside osteopathy is sport, especially rugby union. He played at London Welsh from 1992 to 2010, retiring through injury at 44. He has served as Head of Therapy at Old Priorian RFC (9 seasons) and London Welsh RFC (4 seasons), providing osteopathic, rehab and match-day pitch-side services. He now works with Old Penarthians RFC as Head of Therapy.

His personal experience recovering from injuries brought him to osteopathy, and he's passionate about treating sportspeople at all levels — from weekend warriors to professionals. But it's important to note David treats everyone who needs his services, not just athletes.

Treatments

How We Can Help You

A comprehensive range of manual therapies tailored to your individual needs and goals.

Osteopathy

Holistic manual treatment addressing the root cause of pain. Effective for back and neck pain, postural strains, headaches, and work-related injuries.

Sports Injury & Rehabilitation

Expert assessment and treatment of sports injuries with tailored rehabilitation programmes to get you back to full fitness and prevent recurrence.

Modern Acupuncture

Western acupuncture targeting trigger points in muscles to relieve pain, reduce tension and promote the body's natural healing response.

Active Release Techniques

A patented soft tissue movement-based massage technique for treating problems with muscles, tendons, ligaments, fascia and nerves caused by overuse.

Sports & Remedial Massage

Deep tissue manipulation to reduce pain, speed recovery, break down scar tissue and restore optimal function to muscles and soft tissues.

Kinesio Taping

Therapeutic taping technique to support injured muscles and joints while maintaining full range of movement during recovery and training.
